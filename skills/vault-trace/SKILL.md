# Vault Trace

Trace how an idea has evolved across the user's Obsidian vault over time.

## Steps

1. Ask the user what idea, topic, or decision they want to trace (if not already provided).
2. Use the Obsidian MCP to search the vault for all notes related to that topic.
3. Pay special attention to daily_notes/ for chronological context.
4. Sort findings chronologically. Identify:
   - When the idea first appeared
   - How it changed or developed
   - Contradictions or reversals in thinking
   - Where it currently stands
5. Present as a timeline of thought evolution, not just a list of notes.
6. Surface any unresolved tensions or open questions.

## Output Format

- Timeline of key moments in the idea's evolution
- Turning points: what changed and what triggered it
- Current state of thinking
- Open questions worth exploring further
